About BrothCalm

BrothCalm is a free resource dedicated to sharing the traditions of Chinese food therapy (食疗 — shí liáo). Our name reflects the simple, nourishing power of a warm bowl of broth — comfort food that also happens to be medicine.

Our Mission

We believe that food is your first and most accessible medicine. For over two thousand years, Traditional Chinese Medicine has developed a sophisticated understanding of how different foods affect the body's energy, organs, and overall balance. Our goal is to make this wisdom accessible to anyone curious about eating for wellness — whether you're a complete newcomer to TCM or looking to deepen your practice.

What You'll Find Here

  • Ingredient Deep Dives — Learn about individual foods like goji berries, ginger, jujube dates, astragalus, and more. Their energetic properties, traditional uses, and how to prepare them.
  • Teas & Tonics — Herbal tea recipes for calming the mind, warming the body, and supporting digestion.
  • Recipes — Complete dishes like congee, bone broth, and herbal chicken soup, with step-by-step instructions.
  • Food Therapy Guides — What to eat for anxiety, sleep, digestion, energy, and seasonal wellness.
  • Five Flavors & Theory — The foundational principles of TCM dietary therapy explained in plain language.

Our Approach

We write in the tradition of TCM — citing classical references and time-honored practices rather than trending wellness fads. Every article includes practical instructions so you can try these foods in your own kitchen. We believe in transparency: this is a complementary practice and does not replace professional medical care.
